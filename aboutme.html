about me

Ever since I have my own consciousness, I’m always creating art.
From scribbles to brush strokes, my bedroom walls to textbook pages are conquered by that creativeness.
When I’m bored, I create art to fill up the empty minutes. When my blood is boiling, I create art to calm myself down.
When I’m on cloud nine, I create art to celebrate the joy.
When watching the colors dance on the blank space, there’s only me, my pen, and my canvas.
My works are mostly narrative and emotional. Behind each piece, there’s a story, and behind those stories are different parts of my inner self.
I have various sketchbooks filled with small illustrations as my daily diary.
From daily happenings to significant events, they become scenes of the concepts I want to convey and manifest on my canvas.
However, I want my art to be something that can make people smile.
I want my art to be the medium that transforms frowns into grins as the sun that dries up the rain.
I want to have the power to cure one’s emotions and spread the warmth through the viewer’s heart.
