about me

hello! This is Gracy~ I'm someone with huge passion of art.
"Art is something that makes people smile" is what I always believe, it's also my driving force of creating arts.
Like other artists, I'm in love with art ever since I was young.
When I'm creating my artworks, there's nothing else but me, paper, and pen.
by scribbling those lines, connecting them into shapes...
I can somehow find peace within them.
In my future I would like to pursue illustration, art education,
and maybe art therapy as my major and minors.
here's one of my art accounts where I post mostly graphic design artworks,
feel free to follow it~
